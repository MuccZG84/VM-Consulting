About Me

Hi, I’m Vladimir — a front-end developer with a rich background in electronics and technical troubleshooting. After 17+ years working hands-on with digital printing systems, battery-powered vehicles, and mobile diagnostics, I transitioned into web development to bring elegant, responsive solutions to life.

Today, I specialize in building responsive, elegant websites using HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript (ES6+), and React.js. I’m passionate about clean code, modular design, and creating intuitive user experiences. Whether it’s a multi-page portfolio, a landing page for a client, or a dynamic React app, I bring structure, clarity, and visual polish to every project.

My strengths include:

  • Responsive design and mobile-first layouts
  • Component-based development with React
  • Version control with Git and GitHub
  • UX/UI principles and accessibility best practices
  • Rapid prototyping and iterative troubleshooting

I also bring a deep understanding of technical documentation, presentation design, and client communication — skills honed through years of fieldwork and collaboration. I’m detail-oriented, adaptable, and committed to delivering work that reflects both my standards and your goals.

If you're looking for a developer who blends technical precision with creative vision, I’d love to connect.
